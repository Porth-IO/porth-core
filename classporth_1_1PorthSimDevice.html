Detailed Description

The high-fidelity Digital Twin for Cardiff Photonics.

This class simulates the physical, electrical, and protocol-level behaviors of a compound semiconductor device on a PCIe Gen 6 bus. It manages a background physics thread that models thermal drift and power rail noise.

  • Note
    This is the Sovereign testing ground; it allows for "Chaos Engineering" by injecting hardware-level failures that would be dangerous or impossible to trigger on physical GaN hardware.
