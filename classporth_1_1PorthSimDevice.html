Detailed Description

The high-fidelity Digital Twin for Cardiff Photonics.

This class simulates the physical, electrical, and protocol-level behaviors of a compound semiconductor device on a PCIe Gen 6 bus. It manages a background physics thread that models thermal drift and power rail noise while providing hooks for chaos engineering and protocol-aware register access.
