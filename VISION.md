# Porth-IO Strategic Vision

## The Problem: The "Software Gap"
The South Wales Compound Semiconductor cluster is the world leader in hardware. However, high-frequency trading, AI clusters, and 6G telecommunications require latencies that standard Silicon-based operating systems cannot provide. 

## The Solution: The C++ Bridge
Porth-IO acts as the "Software Bridge." By providing a high-performance, C++ driver stack, we ensure that Cardiff-made chips are "Plug-and-Play" for the world's most demanding low-latency applications.

## Why C++?
We have chosen Modern C++ for its unmatched control over hardware memory-mapping and its deep compatibility with existing industrial and financial infrastructure.
