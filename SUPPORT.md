## 🌟 Support Tiers

### 1. Community Support (Free)

* **GitHub Issues:** For bug reports, feature requests, and technical questions. We aim to acknowledge high-priority bugs within 72 hours.
* **Discussions:** For architectural brainstorming and community knowledge sharing.
* **Documentation:** Detailed guides are available in the `/docs` directory and via our automated Doxygen portal.

### 2. Commercial & Professional Support (Enterprise)

For organizations requiring guaranteed response times and dedicated engineering resources:

* **Service Level Agreements (SLAs):** Sub-4 hour response times for critical production outages.
* **Custom Driver Development:** Bespoke logic layer integration for specific Indium Phosphide (InP) or Gallium Nitride (GaN) ASIC designs.
* **Performance Audits:** On-site or remote deep-dives into your P99 tail latency metrics.
* **Inquiries:** Please contact `harridavies03@gmail.com` with the subject "[Enterprise Support]".

## 🛡️ Security Support

If you have discovered a security vulnerability, please refer to our **SECURITY.md** for our coordinated disclosure process. **Do not open a public issue.**

---

## 📈 Versioning Policy

Porth-IO follows **Semantic Versioning 2.0.0 (SemVer)**:

* **Major:** Breaking API changes (e.g., changing the `PorthRegister` memory layout).
* **Minor:** New functionality in a backwards-compatible manner (e.g., adding a new photonic domain model).
* **Patch:** Backwards-compatible bug fixes and performance micro-optimizations.
