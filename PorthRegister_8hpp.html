constexpr size_t porth::PORTH_CACHE_LINE_SIZE = 64
 The Cardiff Standard Cache Line (64 Bytes). This constant is the physical foundation of our MMIO map. We hardcode 64 bytes to align with the Newport Cluster's PCIe TLP (Transaction Layer Packet) granularity. This prevents "Cache Line Bouncing" between the CPU and the InP/GaN DMA engine, ensuring deterministic register access.
